Generator that cuts sequence (bytes, memoryview, etc.)
   into chunks of given size. If `seq` length is not multiply
   of `size`, the lengh of the last chunk returned will be
   less than requested.

   >>> list( chunks([1,2,3,4,5,6,7], 3) )
   [[1, 2, 3], [4, 5, 6], [7]]
